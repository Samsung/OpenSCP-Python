  • pk_sd_ecka_bytes – public key of the SD used for key agreement in encoded form (PK.SD.ECKA)

  • cert_chain_oce_ecka – certificate chain including the certificate containing the public key of the OCE used for key agreement in encoded form (CERT.OCE.ECKA)

  • scp_mode – SCP mode - S8 or S16
